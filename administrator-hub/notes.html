Welcome to the Z Laboratories computer system! Congratulations on your position of System Administrator.

Our network is equipped with state-of-the-art hardware. Its smooth operation is ensured by advanced automated control software. Should a problem arise, the Direct Connect (TM) protocol developed by our engineers would help you to troubleshoot it without leaving you workstation.

Your responsibilities include monitoring the state of the network and addressing potential issues before they adversely affect research workstations and data storage and retrieval systems.

During your probation period, we expect you to fully familiarize yourself with the network structure.

Your starting point should be feef.0000, which contains an up-to-date listing of main hubs and data stations, as well as notes left by previous system administators. Use the Connect tool available in the Administrator Hub to reach it.
